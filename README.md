# Movie Maker

Create stunning video compilations from your favorite moments.

![Logo](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/okekedev/MovieMaker/main/logo_icon.png)


## Features

- **Easy Video Selection**: Pick multiple videos from your Photos library
- **Intuitive Trimming**: Drag handles to trim start and end points
- **Slow Motion Effects** (Pro): Add cinematic slow-mo to any part of your videos
- **Background Music** (Pro): Add songs from your Apple Music library
- **Smart Orientation**: Auto-adjusts for landscape, portrait, or square videos
- **Audio Control**: Mute individual videos or all at once
- **Reorder Videos**: Hold and drag to rearrange your sequence

## Privacy

Movie Maker is built with privacy in mind:
- No data collection
- No tracking or analytics
- All processing happens locally on your device
- Your videos never leave your device
