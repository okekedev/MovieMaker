Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a video compilation?

1. Tap the plus button on the home screen
2. Select multiple videos from your Photos library
3. Tap each video to trim or edit it
4. Configure your video settings (orientation, music, etc.)
5. Tap "Create Video" to compile your videos

How do I trim or edit videos?

Tap on any selected video thumbnail to open the trimming view. You can drag the handles to trim the start and end, and enable slow-motion effects if you have Pro.

Can I reorder my videos?

Yes! Hold and drag any video thumbnail to rearrange the order before creating your compilation.

How do I add background music?

Background music is a Pro feature. Subscribe to Pro, then in the settings view before creating your video, tap "Select Music" to choose a song from your Apple Music library.

How do I mute individual videos?

When editing a video in the trimming view, toggle the "Mute Video" switch. You can also mute all videos at once from the settings screen.

What's included in the Pro subscription?

  • Background music support
  • Slow-motion effects
  • Unlimited video selections (free plan: 10 videos max)
  • 7-day free trial, then $2.99/month

How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Open Settings on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select Movie Maker Pro
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

How do I restore my purchase?

If you've previously subscribed on this Apple ID, tap "Restore Purchases" on the paywall screen to restore your Pro access.
