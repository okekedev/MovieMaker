Turn your camera roll into a highlight reel your family and friends will actually rewatch.

Movie Maker is the fastest way to combine clips, add photo memories, drop in a soundtrack, and export a finished movie back to your camera roll. Drag, trim, and you're done — no complex timeline, no learning curve, no watermark.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Merge videos from your library into one movie — as many clips as you want.
• Combine videos and photos in any order to build a slideshow with motion.
• Trim the start and end of each clip so only the best moments remain.
• Drag to reorder scenes until the story feels right.
• Add background music and slow-motion to any clip.
• Add per-clip titles and subtitles right on the photo or video.
• Mute clip audio when you want a quiet edit or music-only soundtrack.
• Export back to Photos in full quality — no watermark.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Pick clips and photos from your library.
2. Reorder, trim, and add music or slow-mo.
3. Export and share.

EVERY FEATURE IS FREE
Background music, slow motion, HD export, and no watermark are included for everyone. Coins simply let you save your finished videos — grab a coin pack anytime, or go unlimited with a subscription.

PERFECT FOR
• Travel recaps and vacation highlights
• Family moments and birthday compilations
• Event recaps from weddings, parties, games
• Social posts for Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts
• Quick slideshows from a photo shoot

PRIVACY FIRST
Editing happens entirely on your device. Your media stays in your photo library. Nothing uploads anywhere unless you choose to share.
