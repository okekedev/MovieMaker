Merge videos, photos, and music into one clean movie — no complex timeline.

Movie Maker is the fastest way to combine clips, add a soundtrack, and export to your camera roll. Just drag, trim, and you're done.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Merge videos from your library into one movie — as many clips as you want.
• Combine videos and photos in any order to build a slideshow with motion.
• Trim the start and end of each clip so only the best moments remain.
• Drag to reorder scenes until the story feels right.
• Mute clip audio when you want a quiet edit or music-only soundtrack.
• Export back to Photos in seconds, ready to share.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Pick clips and photos from your library.
2. Reorder, trim, and mute as needed.
3. Add music and export.
