APP REVIEW INFORMATION

TESTING THE APP:

No account or login required. The app works entirely offline with local video processing.

REQUIRED PERMISSIONS:
- Photos Library Access: The app will request permission to access videos from the Photos library. This is required for core functionality (selecting and saving videos).

BASIC WORKFLOW TO TEST:
1. Grant Photos access when prompted
2. Tap the + button on the home screen
3. Select 2-10 videos from Photos library
4. Tap "Continue"
5. Configure settings (orientation, music, mute options)
6. Tap "Create Video"
7. Video will be saved to Photos library automatically

TESTING FREE TIER FEATURES:
- Select up to 10 videos
- Trim individual videos (tap any video thumbnail)
- Rearrange videos (hold and drag thumbnails)
- Mute individual videos or all at once
- Choose landscape, portrait, or square orientation

TESTING PRO FEATURES:
A promotional code has been provided for testing Pro features. Pro features include:
- Background music selection from Apple Music library
- Slow-motion effects on video clips
- Unlimited video selections (more than 10 videos)

VIDEO TRIMMING:
- Tap any selected video thumbnail to open the editor
- Use the playback controls to preview
- Drag the trim handles to adjust start/end points
- Drag slow-mo handles (blue, Pro only) to add slow-motion effects
- Tap "Done" to save changes

PRIVACY & DATA:
- All video processing happens locally on device
- No data is collected, uploaded, or shared
- No network requests except for:
  - Apple Music API (when selecting background music)
  - StoreKit (for in-app purchases)
- Privacy policy: https://okekedev.github.io/MovieMaker/privacy.html

SUBSCRIPTION:

- Managed through StoreKit 2
- Auto-renewable subscription
- Family Sharing enabled

KNOWN LIMITATIONS:
- Requires iOS 17.6 or later
- Works best with videos under 4K resolution
- Processing time depends on video count and device performance
